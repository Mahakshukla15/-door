JAYANTI OLD AGE HOME..

Jayanti Old Age Home has been working to provide shelter and other necessary amenities for destitute abandoned Old and women of the society for years now. Jayanti Old Age Home is registered under the Rajasthan Co-operative Act 1958 under (Section 28, 1958) as of 30 Dec 2003 (Registration No.- 19958/12).Jayanti Old Age Home saw the day of light after the long-cherished desire and ideology by the founder ultimately took a structural shape. Asha Deep is a divine home where orphaned and abandoned Old live in one shelter and an atmosphere where they can wish to have dreams and wish to achieve them. We all are concerned about our own lives and families and do our utmost to keep them healthy and happy, but we should never forget that “HUMANITY IS THE HUMAN RELIGION”.
Jayanti Old Age Home is a thought where we hope for a better future for Old that are filled with human values. No kid should spend their life on the road, and the fragrance of their lives should spread across the world with the noble cause to have a child-friendly environment, system, and society that ensure rights of survival, development, and protection. Jayanti Old Age Home provides them with Safety, Confidence, Family feeling, Opportunities, and a way to fulfill their aspiration & dreams.
